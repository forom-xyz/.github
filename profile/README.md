  <h1 id="save-the-world">SAVE THE WORLD</h1>

  <p><b>Your collective knowledge, your robot.</b></p>
  
  [![🌍Mission](https://img.shields.io/badge/Mission-2b3137?style=for-the-badge&color=FFFFFF)](#save-the-world)


</div>

<div align="justify">
  
  Forom is neither an app nor a traditional social network. It is a social game that you can own, if you have the curiosity for it. We are an open-source initiative driven by a strong conviction: imposing a single centralized model and relying on sprawling data centers, like the FANG+* do, is one of the worst solutions for humanity. It is time to rethink our digital footprint.</p>

  To build a healthier future, we focus on protecting your spaces. By establishing closed communities where access is controlled (by invitation or approval), we guarantee effective moderation that blocks bots, spam, and toxicity. This secure environment allows members to express themselves, share, and collaborate freely, without the fear of being overwhelmed by unwanted noise. The result is true collective building and exchanges of unprecedented relevance.</p>

  Forom takes this logic further by combining closed communities with local self-hosting. You take back total control of your space by creating a true "data castle" that belongs solely to you. Here, moderation is transparent, and the rules are defined by your group. Your collective knowledge remains in your hands, without depending on a third party, without feeding energy-intensive servers, and without ever risking seeing your world erased overnight.</p>

  We provide you with the starter kit, a simplified engine, and game mechanics: it is up to you to invent the missing pieces to set off and conquer space. Your ultimate mission is clear: <b>Save the world</b> by taking back control of our memories and our data. After a year of collaboration and archiving within your castle, your personal ROM will awaken to support you and solve the logical problems that you and your community have created.</p>

</div>
